B.F.A. Programme of 4 year duration under Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) consisting of 8 Semesters of 6 months each

The college offers the following courses leading to the Degree under the R.B.U. Bachelor of Fine Arts (Faculty of Visual Arts) in Painting, Applied Art, Sculpture, Graphic(Print Making). Course duration 4 years(i.e. Eight Semesters) with specialization in Painting/ Sculpture/ Applied Art / Graphic Art (Print Making ) also students will have to take up any one as Elective subjects of 200 marks each as per CBCS syllabus structure of the UGC guidelines. This is an Honours programme equivalent to Honours Degree courses as per UGC regulation and since 1996 the degree, Bachelor of Fine Arts is provided by Rabindra Bharati University, Kolkata.

OURDEPARTMENTS

PAINTING DEPARTMENT

provides training in basic skills in various media like pencil, water color, gouache, oil acrylic and mixed applications etc.

APPLIED ART DEPARTMENT

provides training in publicity advertising and printing activity, such as Typography, Computer Graphic, Indoor & Outdoor Adv etc.

SCULPTURE DEPARTMENT

provides training in study of animal and bird, nature, head in relief and round composition in clay, plaster, cement, wood, stone, metal, fiber etc in differet casting and direct process.

PRINT MAKING DEPARTMENT

provides training in different categories of, lithograph, dry point, etching, aquatin calligraphy etc. relief stencil etc.
